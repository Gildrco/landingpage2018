Gildr was founded on the idea of building brilliant tech, to help communities preserve their legacies and to celebrate life.

We are building sustainable interment models that will support the growing urban needs of our cities while upholding traditions and the environment.

Our approach is to shape better ways our communities share good times and the not so wonderful; while bringing it all down to a science.

We do this to redefine the experiences we go through when someone we love leaves us.
Because every human emotion we experience is a part of us all.
That is why during a time when solace and repose is needed, we want our communities to get to focus on the things that matter most – the people they love and the moments they get to share.
